# VectorCareIQ

**Public site:** [vectorcareiq.com](https://www.vectorcareiq.com)

## Overview

VectorCareIQ is a report-first healthcare market intelligence platform for Business Strategy Analysis, practice evaluation, and area opportunity assessment. It helps analysts and operators evaluate practice opportunities, source evidence, confidence, and diligence needs.
